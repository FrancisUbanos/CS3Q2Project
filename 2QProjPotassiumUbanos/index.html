What is a Nintendo Switch?

The Nintendo Switch is an 8th generation console of Nintendo. Released around the end of 2017 it broke the expectations with around 3 million units shipped in its first month! With popular games such as "The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" "Super Smash Brothers Ultimate", and many more, you and your family can enjoy the many games and possibilities made by this console. With a system capable of being docked in and separate, and having the base console itself capable of 2 people due to its joycons, this unique experience is something you don't get from any modern console to date.
